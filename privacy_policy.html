B. Information from Third-Party Platforms (Instagram & TikTok)

When you connect your Instagram and/or TikTok accounts to our App, you authorize us to collect information through their official Application Programming Interfaces (APIs). We only access the information that is necessary to provide the features you use. This includes:

For Instagram Users:

By connecting your Instagram account (specifically, an Instagram Business or Creator account via the Facebook login), you grant us access to:

  • Profile Information: Your Instagram username, profile picture, bio, and number of followers. We use this to identify your account and display it within the App.
  • Your Content: Your photos, videos, stories, and carousels, along with their captions and comments. We access this to enable you to manage, schedule, and publish content directly from our App.
  • Analytics and Insights: Data about your account and content performance, such as impressions, reach, profile views, website clicks, and audience demographics (e.g., age range, gender, location). We use this to provide you with detailed analytics dashboards and reports.

For TikTok Users:

By connecting your TikTok account, you grant us access to:

  • Profile Information: Your TikTok display name, username, profile picture, and profile description. We use this to identify and display your connected account.
  • Your Public Videos: Information about the videos you have publicly posted, including captions, view counts, like counts, comment counts, and share counts. We use this to allow you to view and manage your content library.
  • Posting Privileges: The ability to upload and post videos directly to your TikTok profile on your behalf, but only when you explicitly schedule or command a post through our App.
  • Analytics Data: Performance metrics for your public videos and profile, including video views, profile views, and follower trends. We use this to power the analytics features of our App.

C. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our App, such as the features you use, the actions you take, and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.
  • Technical Data: We log technical data from your device, including IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information. This helps us with troubleshooting, security, and service improvement.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have rights over your personal data. Depending on your location, these may include the right to:

  • Access, Correct, or Delete your personal information.
  • Object to or Restrict our processing of your data.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke your consent for us to access your platform data at any time.

How to Revoke Access:

You can revoke our App's access to your platform data at any time directly from your Instagram or TikTok account settings.

  • For Instagram: Go to your Instagram profile -> Settings -> Security -> Apps and Websites.
  • For TikTok: Go to your TikTok profile -> Settings and privacy -> Security and login -> Manage app permissions.
Revoking access will prevent us from accessing further data, but we will retain data collected previously in line with our retention policy.
